2022 started off with a bang, with the first LAN since the pandemic – the PGI.S, Two PUBG Continental Series, and the PUBG Global Championship 2022. Overall, a total of 90 teams from all over the globe challenged for the road to the championship, and a total of $15,000,000 worth of prize money has been granted, including $7,000,000 dollars contributed by the fans via crowdfunding. The crowned champion of PGC 2022 will be awarded not only with a total of $1.4 million prize money, honor of the world’s best PUBG esports team, but also a special championship skin to commemorate the victory. PUBG Esports will continue to develop in the upcoming 2022 with global teams and players in order to provide PUBG Esports’ unique entertainment.
